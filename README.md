# Othello Game

A simple implementation of the classic **Othello (Reversi)** board game.

This project allows you to play:
- **Player vs Player** (two humans on the same computer)
- **Player vs AI**

The game follows the official Othello rules where players place pieces to capture the opponent's discs by surrounding them.

---

## Features

- Play against another player or an AI opponent
- Automatic validation of legal moves
- Disc flipping according to Othello rules
- Turn-based gameplay
- Score tracking

---

## How to Play

1. Choose whether to play **against another player or the AI**.
2. Players take turns placing discs on the board.
3. A valid move must capture at least one opponent disc.
4. Captured discs are automatically flipped.
5. The game ends when no valid moves remain.
6. The player with the most discs wins.

---

## Technologies

- Java
- Object-Oriented Programming

---

## Goal

Use strategy to surround your opponent’s discs and capture them.  
Finish the game with **more discs on the board than your opponent**.
